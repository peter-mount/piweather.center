--- type: "manual" title: "solaraltitude" linkTitle: "solaraltitude" description: "Calculate the altitude of the Sun at a location for a specific time" categories: - weather - piweather.center ---

These function's return the altitude or azimuth of the Sun in degrees for a specific time at a location on the Earth.

The solaraltitude function is the more useful of the two, however as the underlying code calculates both at the same time, solarazimuth is also provided.

Syntax

weathercalc

solaraltitude()
solarazimuth()
